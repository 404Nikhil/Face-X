Recognition-Algorithms

In this project, we aim to enhance face recognition capabilities through the implementation of diverse algorithms and frameworks. While numerous open-source face recognition frameworks exist, a comprehensive ready-made solution was not readily available. Consequently, we have taken the initiative to develop and incorporate various algorithms that enable frontal face operations. Unlike existing algorithms that primarily process high-resolution static shots, our implemented algorithms have proven to be highly effective.


Face-Recognition-using-IOT

The emergence of the Internet of Things (IoT) has revolutionized the realm of human activity recognition through the utilization of wearable sensors. This technological advancement offers valuable insights into an individual's functional abilities and lifestyle. By automatically monitoring human activities, personalized support can be provided to cater to the unique needs of diverse individuals.


Facial biometric technology serves the crucial purpose of measuring and comparing distinctive facial characteristics for identification or authentication purposes. By harnessing digital or connected cameras, facial recognition software can effectively detect faces within images, analyze and quantify their specific features, and subsequently compare them to pre-existing templates stored in a database. This powerful technology enables accurate and efficient identification and authentication processes, bolstering security and streamlining various applications across industries.

Face-Mask-Detection

Face Mask Detection employs the power of Artificial Neural Networks to discern whether a user is wearing a mask or not. This innovative technology seamlessly integrates with existing or newly installed IP mask detection cameras, enabling the identification of individuals without masks. App users can conveniently add faces and phone numbers to receive alerts in case of non-compliance. Additionally, if the camera captures an unrecognized face, a prompt notification can be dispatched to the administrator. This comprehensive system ensures heightened safety and adherence to mask-wearing protocols, offering a reliable solution for enforcing mask usage in diverse settings.


Awesome-face-operations

Awesome Face Operations encompass a range of captivating features, including Face Morphing, which seamlessly transforms two input faces into a gradual morph from the first image to the second. Another intriguing capability involves the conversion of an image into a ghostly rendition, achieved through the powerful combination of OpenCV and Numpy. Python, coupled with OpenCV, enables the creation of mesmerizing pencil sketches. Furthermore, Image Segmentation can be achieved using Color space techniques in conjunction with OpenCV. Adding a captivating blurring effect to an image is also among the remarkable functionalities offered. Additionally, the system includes diverse classifications such as age, gender, and many more, providing a comprehensive suite of cutting-edge facial analysis tools.
